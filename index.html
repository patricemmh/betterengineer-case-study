Case Study

Transforming AI Readiness for a Mid-Large Corporation

How BetterEngineer helped Private Corp, an Austin-based company, achieve 30%+ operational efficiency through strategic AI implementation

Challenge

Private Corp had ambitious goals to integrate artificial intelligence (AI) into its operational workflows to boost efficiency, optimize supply chains, and enhance product quality through predictive maintenance. However, leadership faced significant obstacles:

  • Fragmented data infrastructure across multiple systems
  • Limited in-house AI expertise and misaligned talent
  • Unclear vision of how to scale AI initiatives sustainably
  • Concerns about regulatory compliance and ethical AI use
  • Internal conflict around ROI and where to start

"We knew AI could transform our operations, but we were struggling with where to start and how to ensure our investments would deliver real value."

PC
CTO, Private Corp

Solution

BetterEngineer conducted a comprehensive AI Readiness Audit, starting with stakeholder interviews and an in-depth review of Private Corp's data ecosystem. The assessment process included:

Evaluating Existing AI Initiatives

We assessed current AI projects and their alignment with business objectives.

Analyzing Data Infrastructure

Our team identified integration and governance gaps in the existing data systems.

Assessing Team Capabilities

We pinpointed skill gaps and created a talent development strategy.

Providing Tailored Recommendations

We delivered data-driven recommendations for scalable AI adoption.

Deliverables Provided:

  • An actionable AI roadmap outlining short-term and long-term priorities.
  • Detailed risk assessment focusing on data integrity, talent gaps, and compliance.
  • Customized training plans to enhance internal AI capabilities.
  • Executive presentations to align leadership on the strategic AI vision.
